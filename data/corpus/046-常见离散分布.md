## 0-1分布 B(1,p)

- $P(X=1) = p$，$P(X=0) = 1-p$
- $E(X) = p$，$D(X) = p(1-p)$

## 二项分布 B(n,p)

- $P(X=k) = C_n^k p^k (1-p)^{n-k}$
- $E(X) = np$，$D(X) = np(1-p)$
- 可加性：$X_1 \sim B(n_1,p)$，$X_2 \sim B(n_2,p)$，则 $X_1+X_2 \sim B(n_1+n_2,p)$

## 几何分布 G(p)

- $P(X=k) = (1-p)^{k-1}p$
- $E(X) = \frac{1}{p}$，$D(X) = \frac{1-p}{p^2}$

## 泊松分布 P(λ)

- $P(X=k) = \frac{\lambda^k e^{-\lambda}}{k!}$
- $E(X) = \lambda$，$D(X) = \lambda$
- 可加性：$X_1 \sim P(\lambda_1)$，$X_2 \sim P(\lambda_2)$，则 $X_1+X_2 \sim P(\lambda_1+\lambda_2)$

## 典型例题

**例题1**：设 $X \sim P(3)$，求 $P(X=2)$

**解**：$P(X=2) = \frac{3^2 e^{-3}}{2!} = \frac{9 \times e^{-3}}{2} \approx 0.224$
